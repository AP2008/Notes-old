## Autotrophic

- Make own food
- Green leaves b.c. Chlorophyll

## Heterotrophic

### Parisitic
- Food from other  organism
- E.g.:
    * Cuscuta
    * Rafflesia
    * Corpse Flower

### Saprophitic
- Food from dead or decaying subst.
- E.g.:
    * Mushroom
    * Bread mould

### Symbiotic
- Share shelter and food
- E.g.:
    * Rhizobium bacteria & leguminous plants
    * Algae & Fungi => Lichen

### Insectivorous
- Eat insects
- Colourful
- Body parts modified to trap
- Need insects b.c.:
    * Complex form -> simple form (nutrients)
- E.g.:
    * Venus flytrap
