## Types of changes

### Physical Change
- Appearance or form changes
- Subst. remains same
- Physical Characteristics:
    * Lustre
    * Texture
    * Hardness
    * Odour
    * Sounds
    * Temp.
    * Mass
    * Volume
    * Density
    * Color
    * Malleable
    * Ductile
- E.g.:
    * Ice -> Water @ Meltng
    * Water -> Ice @ Cooling

### Chemical Change
- New subst. forms
- Irreversible
- Chemical Properties:
    * flammable
    * corrosive
    * pH
    * explosive
- E.g.:
    * Wood -> carbon @ Burning
    * Iron -> rusted @ Rusting
    * Burning of matchstick @ Combustion
